## What The App Does

NJ Jewellers Gold & Silver Price Tracker is a **branded mobile application** that displays **daily Chennai gold and silver rates** with a premium, professional interface. Customers of NJ Jewellers can open the app every day to instantly see the current market rates.

### Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **22K Gold Rate** | Hero display showing today's 22K gold price per gram (Chennai rate) |
| **24K Gold Rate** | Pure gold price per gram |
| **22K Sovereign** | 8-gram sovereign price (popular purchase unit) |
| **18K Gold Rate** | 18 karat gold price per gram |
| **Silver Per Gram** | Today's Chennai silver rate per gram |
| **Silver Per Kg** | Silver price per kilogram |
| **Price Change** | Green/Red indicators showing how much the price moved from yesterday |
| **7-Day & 30-Day Trend** | Visual sparkline chart showing gold price movement over time |
| **Pull to Refresh** | User can pull down to refresh prices manually |
| **Business Branding** | NJ Jewellers logo, name, and Sowcarpet Chennai location throughout the app |
| **About Section** | Business contact details, phone number, and address from visiting card |
| **Offline Support** | Prices are cached — app works even without internet (shows last fetched rates) |

### Data Accuracy

- Rates are based on **Goodreturns.in Chennai benchmark prices**
- Prices include **import duty (15%)**, **GST (3%)**, and **association premiums**
- These are **retail rates before making charges** (making charges vary per jeweller)
- The app can be upgraded to fetch **live daily rates** via a free API (GoldAPI.io — 100 requests/month free)

### Design

- **Dark luxury theme** — Premium black background with gold accents
- **Two-zone layout** — Gold section (warm gold tones) clearly separated from Silver section (cool silver tones)
- **Minimal and professional** — No clutter, no ads, just prices
- **NJ Jewellers branding** — Logo, business name, and location visible but not overwhelming
- **Works on all screen sizes** — Android phones, iPhones, iPads, tablets
